13.

Working:
a) Number of girls = 40 - 24 = 16
Percentage of girls = (16/40) × 100 = 40%

b) Boys with glasses = 25% of 24 = (25/100) × 24 = 6 boys
Girls with glasses = 50% of 16 = (50/100) × 16 = 8 girls
Total students with glasses = 6 + 8 = 14 students
14.
a) Total profit = ₹930
b) Profit percentage = 24.8%
Working:
Total cost price = 150 × ₹25 = ₹3,750
Selling price of 90 notebooks = 90 × ₹32 = ₹2,880
Remaining notebooks = 150 - 90 = 60
Selling price of 60 notebooks = 60 × ₹28 = ₹1,680
Total selling price = ₹2,880 + ₹1,680 = ₹4,680
Profit = ₹4,680 - ₹3,750 = ₹930
Profit percentage = (930/3750) × 100 = 24.8%
15.
a) Sides are: 21 cm, 28 cm, and 35 cm
b) Yes, it is a right-angled triangle
Working:
a) Let the sides be 3x, 4x, and 5x
Perimeter = 3x + 4x + 5x = 84
12x = 84
x = 7
Therefore, sides are: 3(7) = 21 cm, 4(7) = 28 cm, 5(7) = 35 cm

b) Using Pythagoras theorem (a² + b² = c²):
21² + 28² = 441 + 784 = 1,225
35² = 1,225
Since 21² + 28² = 35², this is a right-angled triangle.
