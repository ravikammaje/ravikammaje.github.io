Section C: Long Answer Questions (5 x 4 = 20 Marks)

Show all your working clearly in your notebook.

1. Rational Numbers:

a) Represent -2/3 and 5/6 on a number line.

b) Arrange the following rational numbers in ascending order: -3/4, 2/3, -5/6, 1/2

c) Find the sum: (-3/7) + (5/14) + (-1/2)

d) The product of two rational numbers is -8/15. If one number is -4/5, find the other number. [4]

2. Profit, Loss, and Percentage:

a) A trader bought a TV for Rs 15,000 and sold it at a profit of 18%. Find the selling price.

b) By selling a bicycle for Rs 4,500, a man loses 10%. What was the cost price of the bicycle?

c) A shirt marked at Rs 800 is sold at Rs 680. Find the discount percentage.

d) If 60% of a number is 144, find the number. [4]

3. Properties of Triangles:

a) Can a triangle have angles measuring 60°, 80°, and 50°? Give reason.

b) In a right-angled triangle, if one acute angle is 35°, find the other acute angle.

c) The exterior angle of a triangle is 110°. If one of the interior opposite angles is 45°, find the other interior opposite angle.

d) In a triangle ABC, angle A = 2x, angle B = 3x, and angle C = 4x. Find all the angles of the triangle. [4]

4. Algebraic Expressions:

a) Add: (3x² + 5x - 7), (2x² - 3x + 4), and (-x² + 2x - 1)

b) Multiply: (2a + 3b) × (4a - 5b)

c) If P = 2x² - 3x + 5 and Q = x² + 4x - 2, find P - Q.

d) Find the value of 3a² - 2ab + b² when a = 2 and b = -3. [4]

5. Data Handling - Mean, Median, Mode:

a) Find the mean marks.

b) Find the median marks.

c) Find the mode of the data.
