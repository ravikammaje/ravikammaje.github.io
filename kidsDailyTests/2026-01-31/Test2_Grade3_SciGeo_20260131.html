Section C: Long Answer Questions (5 x 4 = 20 Marks)

Answer the following questions in detail in your notebook.

1. Our Sense Organs: Science [4]

a) Which sense organ do we use to smell things? What should we not smell?

b) How do our eyes help us? Name two ways we should protect our eyes.

c) Our skin can feel different things. Name four things our skin can feel.

d) Why should we not put sharp objects in our ears?

2. India - Our Country: Geography [4]

a) Name the capital of India. Which river flows through Delhi?

b) Name the highest mountain range in India. In which direction is it located?

c) Name any two famous monuments of India and where they are located.

d) What are the colors in the Indian flag and what does each color represent?

3. Parts of a Plant: Science [4]

a) Draw and label a plant showing: roots, stem, leaves, flower, and fruit.

b) What is the function of the stem in a plant?

c) Name two types of roots with one example of each.

d) What do plants need to make their own food? (Name any three things)

4. Environment and Pollution: Geography [4]

a) What is the environment made up of? Name natural and human-made things in the environment.

b) What is pollution? Name three types of pollution.

c) Write two ways we can reduce air pollution.

d) What are the 3 Rs? How do they help protect the environment?

5. Human Skeletal System: Science [4]

a) What is the job of the skull in our body?

b) What is the backbone made up of? What does it protect?

c) Name the bones found in: (i) our arms (ii) our legs

d) What is a joint? Name two types of joints in our body with examples.
