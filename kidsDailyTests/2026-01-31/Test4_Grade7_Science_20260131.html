Section C: Long Answer Questions (5 x 4 = 20 Marks)

Answer the following questions in detail in your notebook.

1. Sound: Physics [4]

a) What is sound? How is it produced?

b) Explain the difference between loudness and pitch of sound.

c) Why can sound not travel through a vacuum?

d) The speed of sound in air is 340 m/s. If a thunder is heard 5 seconds after a lightning flash, how far is the storm?

2. Atomic Structure: Chemistry [4]

a) Define: (i) Atom (ii) Molecule (iii) Ion

b) Draw the atomic structure of Oxygen (atomic number 8, mass number 16). Show the distribution of electrons.

c) What is valency? Find the valency of Magnesium (atomic number 12).

d) Write the symbols and valencies of: Sodium, Calcium, Chlorine, Oxygen

3. Photosynthesis: Biology [4]

a) Define photosynthesis. Where does it occur in a plant cell?

b) Name the raw materials required for photosynthesis and their sources.

c) What are the factors affecting the rate of photosynthesis?

d) Why are leaves green in color? What happens to plants kept in the dark for a long time?

4. Heat Transfer: Physics [4]

a) Explain the three modes of heat transfer with one example each.

b) Why are cooking utensils made of metal while their handles are made of wood or plastic?

c) Explain how a thermos flask keeps liquids hot or cold for a long time.

d) Why do we wear white clothes in summer and dark clothes in winter?

5. Plant and Animal Tissues: Biology [4]

a) What is the difference between meristematic tissue and permanent tissue?

b) Name and describe two types of permanent plant tissues with their functions.

c) What is epithelial tissue? Where is it found in the human body?

d) Name the tissue that: (i) helps in movement of body parts (ii) carries messages in the body
