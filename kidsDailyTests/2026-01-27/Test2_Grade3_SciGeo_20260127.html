Section C: Long Answer Questions [4 marks each]

Answer the following questions in detail.

1. Our body has a skeletal system made up of bones. Science [4]

a) What does the skeletal system do for our body? (Write two functions)

b) Name the bone that protects our brain.

c) How many bones does an adult human body have?

d) Why should we drink milk for strong bones?

2. India has many different types of physical features. Geography [4]

a) Name the mountain range in the north of India.

b) Name any two deserts found in India.

c) India is surrounded by water on three sides. Name the water bodies on the east, west, and south.

d) Why are rivers important for India?

3. Birds have different types of beaks and feet depending on what they eat and where they live. Science [4]

a) What type of beak does an eagle have and why?

b) What type of feet does a duck have and why?

c) Name a bird that uses its beak to make holes in tree trunks. What does it find there?

d) Why do birds migrate? Name one bird that migrates to India.

4. Pollution is harmful to our environment. Geography [4]

a) What are the three main types of pollution?

b) Give two causes of water pollution.

c) How does air pollution affect our health?

d) Explain what "Reduce, Reuse, Recycle" means with one example for each.

5. Good hygiene and cleanliness keep us healthy. Science [4]

a) Why should we wash our hands before eating?

b) Name two diseases that can spread due to dirty surroundings.

c) Write three healthy habits every student should follow.

d) Why is it important to brush our teeth twice a day?
