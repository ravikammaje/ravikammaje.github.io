A3. Mirror Formula:
Given: f = 15 cm, u = -30 cm (object distance is negative)

A14. Light Phenomena:

Reflection: Bouncing back of light from a surface

Refraction: Bending of light when passing between media of different densities

Total Internal Reflection (TIR): Complete reflection when light travels from denser to rarer medium at angle greater than critical angle

Conditions for TIR: (1) Light must travel from denser to rarer medium, (2) Angle of incidence > critical angle

Applications: Optical fibres, periscopes, binoculars, diamonds' brilliance

A15. Soil Erosion:

Definition: Removal of topsoil by wind, water, or human activities

Causes: Deforestation, overgrazing, improper farming, heavy rainfall, strong winds

Prevention methods: Afforestation and reforestation, Contour ploughing, Terrace farming, Building check dams, Crop rotation

Importance: Preserves soil fertility, maintains water cycle, ensures food security
