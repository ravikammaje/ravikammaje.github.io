Section C: Long Answer Questions (5 x 4 = 20 Marks)

Show all your working clearly in your notebook.

1. Fractions and Operations:

a) Arrange in ascending order: 5/8, 3/4, 7/12, 2/3

b) Find the value of: 2 1/3 × 1 4/5

c) A rope is 8 3/4 meters long. If 3 2/5 meters is cut off, what length remains?

d) Divide: 4 2/3 ÷ 1 1/6 [4]

2. Decimals and Percentages:

a) Calculate: 23.56 × 4.5

b) Convert 0.45 to a percentage and 35% to a decimal.

c) A book costs Rs. 450. If a discount of 15% is given, find the selling price.

d) Express 72 as a percentage of 180. [4]

3. Area and Perimeter:

a) A rectangular field is 85 m long and 60 m wide. Find its area and the cost of fencing it at Rs. 25 per meter.

b) The area of a square is 196 sq cm. Find its perimeter.

c) A rectangular garden has length 24 m and breadth 18 m. A path 2 m wide runs inside along the boundary. Find the area of the path.

d) How many square tiles of side 20 cm are needed to cover a floor of dimensions 4 m × 3 m? [4]

4. Profit, Loss and Simple Interest:

a) A shopkeeper buys a cycle for Rs. 3,500 and sells it for Rs. 4,200. Find the profit and profit percentage.

b) If an item is sold at Rs. 1,080 with a loss of 10%, find the cost price.

c) Calculate the simple interest on Rs. 8,000 at 6% per annum for 2 years 6 months.

d) At what rate of interest will Rs. 5,000 amount to Rs. 5,600 in 2 years? [4]

5. Unitary Method and Time-Distance Problems:

a) If 12 workers can complete a job in 15 days, how many days will 20 workers take to complete the same job?

b) A car travels 234 km using 18 liters of petrol. How many liters are needed to travel 390 km?

c) Two trains start from stations 480 km apart and travel towards each other at speeds of 70 km/hr and 50 km/hr. After how many hours will they meet?

d) The ratio of boys to girls in a class is 5:3. If there are 40 students in total, find the number of boys and girls. [4]
