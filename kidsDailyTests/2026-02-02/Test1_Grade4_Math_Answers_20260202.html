Section B: Short Answer Questions (5 x 2 = 10 Marks)

1. Write 74,089 in words and expanded form

Expanded form: 70,000 + 4,000 + 0 + 80 + 9 OR 70,000 + 4,000 + 80 + 9

Check: 1 x 36, 2 x 18, 3 x 12, 4 x 9, 6 x 6

3. Perimeter of rectangle

Length = 12 cm, Breadth = 7 cm

Perimeter = 2 x (Length + Breadth)

= 2 x (12 + 7) = 2 x 19 = 38 cm

4. Convert 3 hours 45 minutes to minutes

1 hour = 60 minutes

3 hours = 3 x 60 = 180 minutes

3 hours 45 minutes = 180 + 45 = 225 minutes

5. Pattern: 7, 14, 28, 56, ___, ___, ___

Rule: Double the previous number (multiply by 2)

56 x 2 = 112

112 x 2 = 224

224 x 2 = 448

Next three numbers: 112, 224, 448
