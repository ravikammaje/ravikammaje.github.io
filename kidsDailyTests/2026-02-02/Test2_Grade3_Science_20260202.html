Section C: Long Answer Questions (5 x 4 = 20 Marks)

Answer the following questions in detail in your notebook.

1. Living and Non-living Things: Science [4]

a) What are the main characteristics of living things? Name any four.

b) Give two examples each of natural non-living things and man-made non-living things.

c) A car can move. Does that make it a living thing? Explain your answer.

d) Look at this list: Tree, Cloud, Frog, River, Bird, Rock. Separate them into living and non-living things.

2. Food We Get from Plants: Science [4]

a) Name two examples each of: (i) Cereals (ii) Pulses (iii) Oil-giving plants

b) We eat different parts of different plants. Match the plant with the part we eat:

   Potato - ?, Spinach - ?, Orange - ?, Cauliflower - ?

c) What are spices? Name three spices and tell which part of the plant they come from.

d) Why do we need to eat food from plants? Give two reasons.

3. The Sun - Our Star: Science [4]

a) What is the Sun? Why is it important for life on Earth?

b) How does the Sun help in the water cycle? Explain briefly.

c) What is solar energy? Name two uses of solar energy in our daily life.

d) Why should we not look directly at the Sun? What precautions should we take on very sunny days?

4. Cleanliness, Health and Hygiene: Science [4]

a) What is personal hygiene? Name four good habits of personal hygiene.

b) When should we wash our hands? List four important times.

c) How can we keep our surroundings clean? Write three ways.

d) Why is it important to: (i) Cover our mouth when coughing (ii) Drink clean water (iii) Cut nails regularly?
