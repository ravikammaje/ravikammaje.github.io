Section C: Long Answer Questions (5 x 4 = 20 Marks)

Show all your working clearly in your notebook.

1. The Human Body - Circulatory System:

a) What is the main function of the circulatory system?

b) Name the four chambers of the human heart.

c) What is the difference between arteries and veins?

d) Why is the heart called a "double pump"? Explain briefly. [4]

2. Ecosystems and Environment:

a) What is an ecosystem? Give two examples of different ecosystems.

b) Explain the difference between a producer, consumer, and decomposer with examples.

c) What is meant by "biodegradable" and "non-biodegradable" waste? Give two examples of each.

d) How does deforestation affect the environment? List any three effects. [4]

3. Light and Sound:

a) What is the difference between luminous and non-luminous objects? Give two examples of each.

b) Explain how we see objects that do not emit their own light.

c) What is an echo? Under what conditions can we hear an echo?

d) Why can sound not travel through vacuum but light can? [4]

4. Simple Machines and Force:

a) What is a simple machine? Name any four types of simple machines.

b) Explain the working of a lever. What are the three classes of levers? Give one example of each class.

c) How does a pulley make work easier?

d) A boy applies a force of 50 N to push a box 4 meters. Calculate the work done. [4]

5. Earth and Space:

a) Why do we have day and night on Earth? Explain with the concept of rotation.

b) What causes the seasons to change? How is it related to Earth's revolution and tilt?

c) What is the difference between a solar eclipse and a lunar eclipse?

d) Name any four natural satellites and the planets they orbit. [4]
