Section A: Multiple Choice Questions (5 x 2 = 10 Marks)

1. Value of 3/5 + 1/4

Answer: b) 17/20

LCM of 5 and 4 = 20
3/5 = 12/20, 1/4 = 5/20
12/20 + 5/20 = 17/20

2. HCF of 36 and 48

Answer: c) 12

36 = 2² × 3²
48 = 2⁴ × 3
HCF = 2² × 3 = 12

3. Area of square with perimeter 64 cm

Answer: a) 256 sq cm

Side = 64 ÷ 4 = 16 cm
Area = 16 × 16 = 256 sq cm

4. Decimal equivalent of 3/8

Answer: b) 0.375

3 ÷ 8 = 0.375

5. Number with quotient 12, remainder 5 when divided by 7

Answer: b) 89

Number = Divisor × Quotient + Remainder
= 7 × 12 + 5 = 84 + 5 = 89

Section B: Short Answer Questions (5 x 2 = 10 Marks)

1. LCM of 15, 20, and 30 using prime factorization

15 = 3 × 5

20 = 2² × 5

30 = 2 × 3 × 5

LCM = 2² × 3 × 5 = 4 × 3 × 5 = 60

2. Simplify: 4 2/5 - 2 3/4

Convert to improper fractions:

4 2/5 = 22/5 and 2 3/4 = 11/4

LCM of 5 and 4 = 20

22/5 = 88/20 and 11/4 = 55/20

88/20 - 55/20 = 33/20 = 1 13/20

3. Train travels 375 km in 5 hours

Average speed = Distance ÷ Time = 375 ÷ 5 = 75 km/hr

Distance in 8 hours = 75 × 8 = 600 km

4. Convert 0.625 to fraction in lowest terms

0.625 = 625/1000

Divide by HCF (125):

625 ÷ 125 = 5, 1000 ÷ 125 = 8

0.625 = 5/8

5. Sum of three consecutive even numbers is 78

Let the numbers be x, x+2, x+4

x + (x+2) + (x+4) = 78

3x + 6 = 78

3x = 72

x = 24

The numbers are: 24, 26, 28

= 7/3 × 9/5 = 63/15 = 21/5 = 4 1/5

2. Decimals and Percentages:

a) 23.56 × 4.5:

2356 × 45 = 106020

Decimal places = 2 + 1 = 3

Answer = 106.020 or 106.02

b) Conversions:

0.45 to percentage = 0.45 × 100 = 45%

35% to decimal = 35 ÷ 100 = 0.35

c) Book selling price:

Cost = Rs. 450, Discount = 15%

Discount amount = 15% of 450 = 450 × 15/100 = Rs. 67.50

Selling price = 450 - 67.50 = Rs. 382.50

d) 72 as percentage of 180:

= (72/180) × 100 = 7200/180 = 40%

3. Area and Perimeter:

a) Rectangular field (85 m × 60 m):

Area = 85 × 60 = 5,100 sq m

Perimeter = 2 × (85 + 60) = 2 × 145 = 290 m

Cost of fencing = 290 × 25 = Rs. 7,250

b) Square with area 196 sq cm:

Side = √196 = 14 cm

Perimeter = 4 × 14 = 56 cm

c) Path area in garden (24 m × 18 m, path 2 m wide inside):

Outer area = 24 × 18 = 432 sq m

Inner dimensions = (24-4) × (18-4) = 20 × 14 = 280 sq m

Path area = 432 - 280 = 152 sq m

d) Tiles needed (floor 4 m × 3 m, tile 20 cm):

Floor area = 400 cm × 300 cm = 120,000 sq cm

Tile area = 20 × 20 = 400 sq cm

Number of tiles = 120,000 ÷ 400 = 300 tiles

4. Profit, Loss and Simple Interest:

a) Cycle CP = Rs. 3,500, SP = Rs. 4,200:

Profit = SP - CP = 4200 - 3500 = Rs. 700

Profit % = (700/3500) × 100 = 20%

b) SP = Rs. 1,080 with 10% loss:

SP = CP - 10% of CP = 90% of CP

CP = 1080 × 100/90 = Rs. 1,200

c) SI on Rs. 8,000 at 6% for 2.5 years:

SI = (P × R × T)/100

SI = (8000 × 6 × 2.5)/100 = 120000/100 = Rs. 1,200

d) Rate for Rs. 5,000 to become Rs. 5,600 in 2 years:

SI = 5600 - 5000 = Rs. 600

R = (SI × 100)/(P × T) = (600 × 100)/(5000 × 2)

R = 60000/10000 = 6% per annum

5. Unitary Method and Time-Distance Problems:

a) 12 workers in 15 days, 20 workers = ?

Total work = 12 × 15 = 180 worker-days

Days for 20 workers = 180 ÷ 20 = 9 days

b) 234 km in 18 liters, 390 km = ?

1 km needs = 18/234 liters

390 km needs = (18/234) × 390 = 7020/234 = 30 liters

c) Trains 480 km apart, speeds 70 and 50 km/hr:

Relative speed = 70 + 50 = 120 km/hr

Time to meet = 480 ÷ 120 = 4 hours

d) Ratio of boys to girls = 5:3, total = 40:

Sum of ratio = 5 + 3 = 8

Boys = (5/8) × 40 = 25 boys

Girls = (3/8) × 40 = 15 girls
