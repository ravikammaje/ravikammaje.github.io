Section A: Multiple Choice Questions (5 x 2 = 10 Marks)

1. Place value of 7 in 4,753

Answer: c) 700

7 is in the hundreds place, so its place value is 700.

2. 9 x 7 = ?

Answer: b) 63

9 x 7 = 63

3. Lines of symmetry in a square

Answer: c) 4

A square has 4 lines of symmetry (2 diagonal + 2 through midpoints).

4. 3 km = ? meters

Answer: c) 3,000 m

1 km = 1,000 m, so 3 km = 3,000 m

5. Pattern: 5, 10, 20, 40, ___

Answer: c) 80

Rule: Double the previous number. 40 x 2 = 80

Section B: Short Answer Questions (5 x 2 = 10 Marks)

1. Number name and expanded form for 6,409

Number name: Six thousand four hundred nine

Expanded form: 6,000 + 400 + 0 + 9 OR 6,000 + 400 + 9

2. Pencils in 12 boxes

One box = 8 pencils

12 boxes = 8 x 12 = 96 pencils

3. Rectangle - Lines of symmetry

A rectangle has 2 lines of symmetry.

One horizontal line through the middle and one vertical line through the middle.

4. 2 kg 500 g in grams

1 kg = 1,000 g

2 kg = 2,000 g

2 kg 500 g = 2,000 + 500 = 2,500 g

5. Pattern: 3, 6, 9, 12, ___, ___

Rule: Add 3 each time (multiples of 3)

Next two numbers: 15, 18

Section C: Long Answer Questions (5 x 4 = 20 Marks)

1. Place Value and Number Names:

a) Greatest 4-digit number with 3, 7, 1, 9: Arrange in descending order: 9,731

b) Smallest 4-digit number with 3, 7, 1, 9: Arrange in ascending order: 1,379

c) Arranging from smallest to largest:

Compare thousands (all 5), then hundreds: 2 < 3 < 6

Order: 5,236 ; 5,362 ; 5,623 ; 5,632

d) In 3,527:

Face value of 5 = 5

Place value of 5 = 500 (5 is in hundreds place)

Difference = 500 - 5 = 495

2. Multiplication Word Problems:

a) Chocolates: 6 x 11 = 66 chocolates

b) Chairs in hall: 12 x 8 = 96 chairs

c) 47 x 6:

Step 1: 7 x 6 = 42, write 2, carry 4

Step 2: 4 x 6 = 24, add 4 = 28

Answer: 282

d) Cost of 9 books: 35 x 9 = Rs 315

3. 2D Shapes and Symmetry:

a) Shapes:

(i) 3 sides: Triangle

(ii) 5 sides: Pentagon

(iii) 6 sides: Hexagon

b) Circle: A circle has infinite (unlimited) lines of symmetry. Any line through the center is a line of symmetry.

c) Letters with vertical line of symmetry: A, H, M, T (B does not have vertical symmetry)

d) Equilateral triangle: Has 3 lines of symmetry - one from each vertex to the midpoint of the opposite side.

4. Measurement - Length and Weight:

a) Conversions:

(i) 4 km 250 m = 4,000 + 250 = 4,250 m

(ii) 5,680 m = 5,000 m + 680 m = 5 km 680 m

b) Total distance walked:

1 km 350 m + 2 km 700 m

= 3 km 1,050 m = 4 km 50 m

c) Weight conversions:

(i) 3 kg 750 g = 3,000 + 750 = 3,750 g

(ii) 6,200 g = 6,000 g + 200 g = 6 kg 200 g

d) Watermelon vs 5 mangoes:

5 mangoes = 5 x 350 g = 1,750 g = 1 kg 750 g

Watermelon = 4 kg 200 g = 4,200 g

Difference = 4,200 - 1,750 = 2,450 g = 2 kg 450 g

c) 7, 14, ___, 28, 35, ___
