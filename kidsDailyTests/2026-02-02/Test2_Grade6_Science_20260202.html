Section C: Long Answer Questions (5 x 4 = 20 Marks)

Show all your working clearly in your notebook.

1. The Cell - Structure and Function:

a) What is a cell? Who discovered cells and in which year?

b) Name two organelles found only in plant cells but not in animal cells.

c) State the function of: (i) Nucleus (ii) Cell membrane (iii) Vacuole

d) Draw a well-labeled diagram of a plant cell showing at least 5 parts. [4]

2. Magnetism:

a) What is a magnetic field? How can you show that a magnetic field exists around a magnet?

b) Explain any two methods of making a magnet.

c) What happens when you break a bar magnet into two pieces?

d) State three ways by which a magnet can lose its magnetism (demagnetization). [4]

3. Elements, Compounds and Mixtures:

a) Differentiate between a compound and a mixture (any three differences).

b) Classify the following as element, compound, or mixture: (i) Air (ii) Water (iii) Iron (iv) Sugar

c) Write the symbols for: Oxygen, Nitrogen, Carbon, Calcium, Sodium

d) Explain the separation technique of sublimation with an example. [4]

4. The Circulatory System:

a) Name the four chambers of the human heart and state which side receives oxygenated blood.

b) Differentiate between arteries and veins (any three differences).

c) What are the four blood groups? Which blood group is called the universal donor and why?

d) What is blood pressure? What are healthy practices to keep the heart healthy? [4]

5. Light:

a) What is meant by rectilinear propagation of light? Give one example to prove it.

b) Explain how shadows are formed. What are the conditions necessary for shadow formation?

c) How does a pinhole camera work? Draw a simple diagram.

d) Why is the shadow of an object always dark, regardless of the color of the object? [4]
