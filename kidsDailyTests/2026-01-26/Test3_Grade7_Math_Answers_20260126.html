Section A: Multiple Choice Questions (5 × 2 = 10 Marks)

1. What is the value of (-8) × (-5)?

Answer: b) 40

Negative × Negative = Positive. So (-8) × (-5) = +40

2. Express 0.375 as a fraction in lowest terms.

Answer: a) 3/8

0.375 = 375/1000 = 75/200 = 15/40 = 3/8

3. If 2x + 5 = 17, what is the value of x?

Answer: b) 6

2x + 5 = 17 → 2x = 17 - 5 = 12 → x = 6

4. What is the value of 3⁴?

Answer: d) 81

3⁴ = 3 × 3 × 3 × 3 = 9 × 9 = 81

5. Two angles are supplementary. If one angle is 65°, what is the other angle?

Answer: c) 115°

Supplementary angles add up to 180°. Other angle = 180° - 65° = 115°

Section B: Short Answer Questions (5 × 2 = 10 Marks)

1. Simplify: (-12) + 18 + (-7) + (-3) + 14 [2]

Step 1: Group positive and negative numbers

Positive: 18 + 14 = 32

Negative: (-12) + (-7) + (-3) = -22

Step 2: 32 + (-22) = 32 - 22 = 10

Answer: 10

2. Find the value of: 2⁵ × 2³ ÷ 2⁴ [2]

Using laws of exponents:

aᵐ × aⁿ = aᵐ⁺ⁿ and aᵐ ÷ aⁿ = aᵐ⁻ⁿ

2⁵ × 2³ ÷ 2⁴ = 2⁵⁺³⁻⁴ = 2⁴ = 16

Answer: 16

3. A shopkeeper buys an article for ₹450 and sells it for ₹540. Find the profit percentage. [2]

Cost Price (CP) = ₹450

Selling Price (SP) = ₹540

Profit = SP - CP = 540 - 450 = ₹90

Profit % = (Profit/CP) × 100 = (90/450) × 100 = 20%

Answer: 20%

4. Find the perimeter of a rectangle whose length is 12 cm and whose area is 84 cm². [2]

Area = length × width

84 = 12 × width

Width = 84/12 = 7 cm

Perimeter = 2(length + width) = 2(12 + 7) = 2 × 19 = 38 cm

Answer: 38 cm

5. In a triangle, one angle is 45° and another is 75°. Find the third angle. What type of triangle is this based on its angles? [2]

Sum of angles in a triangle = 180°

Third angle = 180° - 45° - 75° = 60°

All angles are less than 90°, so it is an acute-angled triangle.

Answer: Third angle = 60°; Acute-angled triangle

Section C: Problem Solving (5 × 4 = 20 Marks)

1. A sum of ₹8,000 is deposited at simple interest for 3 years. If the total amount after 3 years is ₹9,440, find:
a) The simple interest earned
b) The rate of interest per annum [4]

Given: Principal (P) = ₹8,000, Time (T) = 3 years, Amount (A) = ₹9,440

a) Simple Interest:

SI = Amount - Principal = ₹9,440 - ₹8,000 = ₹1,440

b) Rate of Interest:

SI = (P × R × T)/100

1,440 = (8,000 × R × 3)/100

1,440 = 24,000R/100 = 240R

R = 1,440/240 = 6%

Answer: a) Simple Interest = ₹1,440; b) Rate = 6% per annum

2. In a class of 40 students, 25% are girls. If 4 more girls join the class, what is the new percentage of girls in the class? [4]

Step 1: Find original number of girls

Girls = 25% of 40 = (25/100) × 40 = 10 girls

Step 2: Find new numbers after 4 girls join

New number of girls = 10 + 4 = 14 girls

New total students = 40 + 4 = 44 students

Step 3: Calculate new percentage

New percentage = (14/44) × 100 = 1400/44 = 31.82% (or 350/11 %)

Answer: New percentage of girls ≈ 31.82% (or 31 9/11%)

3. A car travels 240 km in 4 hours. [4]

a) Speed:

Speed = Distance/Time = 240/4 = 60 km/h

b) Time to travel 420 km:

Time = Distance/Speed = 420/60 = 7 hours

c) Distance in 7 hours:

Distance = Speed × Time = 60 × 7 = 420 km

Answer: a) 60 km/h; b) 7 hours; c) 420 km

4. Solve the following equations: [4]

a) 5x - 3 = 2x + 12

5x - 2x = 12 + 3 (transposing)

3x = 15

x = 15/3 = 5

Verification: LHS = 5(5) - 3 = 22; RHS = 2(5) + 12 = 22 ✓

b) (3y + 7)/4 = 10

3y + 7 = 10 × 4 = 40 (cross multiplying)

3y = 40 - 7 = 33

y = 33/3 = 11

Verification: (3×11 + 7)/4 = 40/4 = 10 ✓

Answer: a) x = 5; b) y = 11

5. Mean, Median, Mode calculation [4]

Data: 45, 52, 38, 65, 52, 48, 52, 70, 42, 56

a) Mean:

Sum = 45 + 52 + 38 + 65 + 52 + 48 + 52 + 70 + 42 + 56 = 520

Mean = Sum/Number of values = 520/10 = 52

b) Median:

Arranged in order: 38, 42, 45, 48, 52, 52, 52, 56, 65, 70

n = 10 (even), so Median = (5th value + 6th value)/2 = (52 + 52)/2 = 52

c) Mode:

52 appears 3 times (most frequent)

Mode = 52

Answer: a) Mean = 52; b) Median = 52; c) Mode = 52
