Section A: Multiple Choice Questions (5 × 2 = 10 Marks)

1. What is the place value of 7 in 4,758?

Answer: c) 700

The 7 is in the hundreds place, so its place value is 7 × 100 = 700

2. 9 × 7 = ?

Answer: b) 63

9 × 7 = 63 (multiplication table of 9)

3. Which shape has exactly 4 equal sides and 4 right angles?

Answer: b) Square

A square has 4 equal sides and all angles are 90°

4. How many minutes are there in 2 hours and 15 minutes?

Answer: c) 135 minutes

2 hours = 2 × 60 = 120 minutes; 120 + 15 = 135 minutes

5. What is the next number in the pattern: 5, 10, 20, 40, ___?

Answer: c) 80

Pattern: Each number is doubled (×2). 40 × 2 = 80

Section B: Short Answer Questions (5 × 2 = 10 Marks)

1. Write the number name for 6,209. [2]

Answer: Six thousand two hundred nine

6,209 = 6 thousands + 2 hundreds + 0 tens + 9 ones

2. Arrange in ascending order: 3,456 ; 3,654 ; 3,546 ; 3,465 [2]

Answer: 3,456 < 3,465 < 3,546 < 3,654

Ascending means smallest to largest. Compare from thousands, then hundreds, then tens.

3. A pencil costs ₹8. How much will 12 pencils cost? [2]

Answer: ₹96

Cost of 12 pencils = 12 × ₹8 = ₹96

4. 84 ÷ 7 = ? Show your working. [2]

Answer: 12

84 ÷ 7 = 12 (because 7 × 12 = 84)

5. How many lines of symmetry does a rectangle have? [2]

Answer: 2 lines of symmetry

A rectangle has 2 lines of symmetry - one vertical through the middle and one horizontal through the middle.

Section C: Problem Solving (5 × 4 = 20 Marks)

1. A shop has 2,485 red balls and 1,678 blue balls. How many balls are there in total? If 1,250 balls are sold, how many balls are left? [4]

Step 1: Total balls = Red balls + Blue balls

= 2,485 + 1,678 = 4,163 balls

Step 2: Balls left = Total balls - Balls sold

= 4,163 - 1,250 = 2,913 balls

Answer: Total = 4,163 balls; Balls left = 2,913 balls

2. There are 8 boxes. Each box contains 11 chocolates. If these chocolates are shared equally among 4 children, how many chocolates will each child get? [4]

Step 1: Total chocolates = Number of boxes × Chocolates per box

= 8 × 11 = 88 chocolates

Step 2: Chocolates per child = Total chocolates ÷ Number of children

= 88 ÷ 4 = 22 chocolates

Answer: Each child gets 22 chocolates

3. Riya's mother gave her ₹100 to buy fruits. She bought 3 apples at ₹15 each and 2 bananas at ₹5 each. How much money did she spend? How much money is left with her? [4]

Step 1: Cost of apples = 3 × ₹15 = ₹45

Step 2: Cost of bananas = 2 × ₹5 = ₹10

Step 3: Total spent = ₹45 + ₹10 = ₹55

Step 4: Money left = ₹100 - ₹55 = ₹45

Answer: Money spent = ₹55; Money left = ₹45

4. A rectangular garden is 12 meters long and 8 meters wide. What is the perimeter of the garden? If a fence costs ₹25 per meter, what is the total cost of fencing the garden? [4]

Step 1: Perimeter of rectangle = 2 × (length + width)

= 2 × (12 + 8) = 2 × 20 = 40 meters

Step 2: Total cost = Perimeter × Cost per meter

= 40 × ₹25 = ₹1,000

Answer: Perimeter = 40 meters; Total cost = ₹1,000
