Section C: Long Answer Questions (5 x 4 = 20 Marks)

Answer the following questions in detail.

1. Plants have different parts that help them survive. Science [4]

a) Draw a plant and label its four main parts: root, stem, leaf, and flower.

b) What is the function of the stem?

c) How do roots help the plant?

d) Why do leaves have tiny holes called stomata?

2. India has many beautiful and historical monuments. Geography [4]

a) Who built the Taj Mahal and why?

b) Name the monument in Delhi where our national flag is hoisted on Independence Day.

c) What is the Gateway of India famous for? In which city is it located?

d) Name one ancient temple in India and tell where it is located.

3. The Sun is called the natural source of light and energy. Science [4]

a) What is solar energy? Name two ways we use solar energy.

b) Why do we have day and night? (Explain using the rotation of Earth)

c) What would happen if there was no Sun?

d) Name two things in nature that need sunlight to survive.

4. Earth experiences different seasons during the year. Geography [4]

a) Why do we have different seasons? (Hint: Earth's revolution around the Sun)

b) Describe the weather during summer season in India.

c) What happens during the monsoon season? Why is it important for farmers?

d) In which season do we wear woolen clothes? Why?

5. Plants provide us with many useful things. Science [4]

a) Name three fruits and three vegetables we get from plants.

b) What do we get from the stem of sugarcane?

c) Name two spices and the plant parts they come from.

d) How are plants helpful for the environment? Give two reasons.
