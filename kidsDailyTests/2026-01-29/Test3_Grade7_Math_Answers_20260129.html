1. Express 3/4 as a percentage.

Answer: b) 75%

Answer: c) 7/9

Section B: Short Answer Questions (5 x 2 = 10 Marks)

1. Simplify: (-3/5) + (2/3) - (-1/15) [2]

Answer:

LCM of 5, 3, 15 = 15

= (-9/15) + (10/15) + (1/15)

= (-9 + 10 + 1)/15

= 2/15

2. Convert 0.375 to a fraction in its lowest terms. [2]

Answer:

0.375 = 375/1000

= 375/1000 (divide both by 125)

= 3/8

3. A shirt marked at Rs 800 with 15% discount. Find selling price. [2]

Answer:

Discount = 15% of 800 = (15/100) x 800 = Rs 120

Selling Price = 800 - 120 = Rs 680

4. Find the area of a parallelogram (base 15 cm, height 9 cm). [2]

Answer:

Area of parallelogram = base x height

= 15 x 9

= 135 cm2

5. Coin tossed once: (i) P(head), (ii) P(not a tail) [2]

Answer:

(i) P(head) = 1/2 (one favorable outcome out of two possible)

(ii) P(not a tail) = P(head) = 1/2

Section C: Long Answer Questions (5 x 4 = 20 Marks)

1. Rational Numbers [4]

a) Arrange in ascending order: -2/3, 5/6, -3/4, 1/2

LCM of 3, 6, 4, 2 = 12

-2/3 = -8/12, 5/6 = 10/12, -3/4 = -9/12, 1/2 = 6/12

Ascending order: -9/12 < -8/12 < 6/12 < 10/12

Answer: -3/4 < -2/3 < 1/2 < 5/6

b) Find: (-2/5) x (3/7) + (-2/5) x (4/7)

Using distributive property: (-2/5) x [(3/7) + (4/7)]

= (-2/5) x (7/7)

= (-2/5) x 1

= -2/5

c) Product = -8/9, one number = -4/3, find the other

Let the other number be x

(-4/3) x x = -8/9

x = (-8/9) / (-4/3) = (-8/9) x (-3/4)

x = 24/36 = 2/3

d) Rational number between 1/4 and 1/3

Method: (a + b)/2

= (1/4 + 1/3)/2 = (3/12 + 4/12)/2 = (7/12)/2 = 7/24

(Also acceptable: 5/18, 6/20, etc.)

2. Percentage, Profit and Loss [4]

a) 35% of a number is 175. Find the number.

Let the number be x

35% of x = 175

(35/100) x x = 175

x = 175 x (100/35) = 175 x (20/7) = 500

b) CP = Rs 5,000, SP = Rs 4,500. Find loss%.

Loss = CP - SP = 5000 - 4500 = Rs 500

Loss% = (Loss/CP) x 100 = (500/5000) x 100 = 10%

c) SP = Rs 720 with 20% gain. Find CP.

SP = CP + 20% of CP = 120% of CP

720 = (120/100) x CP

CP = 720 x (100/120) = Rs 600

d) What percentage is 45 minutes of 3 hours?

3 hours = 180 minutes

Percentage = (45/180) x 100 = (1/4) x 100 = 25%

3. Area of Plane Figures [4]

a) Area of rectangular park (80 m x 60 m):

Area = length x breadth = 80 x 60 = 4,800 m2

b) Area of triangular flower bed (base 20 m, height 15 m):

Area = (1/2) x base x height = (1/2) x 20 x 15 = 150 m2

c) Area of circular fountain (radius 7 m):

Area = pi x r2 = (22/7) x 7 x 7 = 22 x 7 = 154 m2

d) Remaining area:

Total park area = 4,800 m2

Area used = flower bed + fountain = 150 + 154 = 304 m2

Remaining area = 4,800 - 304 = 4,496 m2

4. Algebraic Expressions and Equations [4]

a) Simplify: 3(2x - 5) - 2(4x - 3) + 5x

= 6x - 15 - 8x + 6 + 5x

= 6x - 8x + 5x - 15 + 6

= 3x - 9

b) Solve: (3x - 2)/4 - (2x + 1)/3 = 1

Multiply by LCM (12):

3(3x - 2) - 4(2x + 1) = 12

9x - 6 - 8x - 4 = 12

x - 10 = 12

x = 22

c) Sum of three consecutive even numbers = 78

Let the numbers be x, x+2, x+4

x + (x+2) + (x+4) = 78

3x + 6 = 78

3x = 72

x = 24

The numbers are 24, 26, and 28

d) If 2x + 3y = 13 and x = 2, find y:

2(2) + 3y = 13

4 + 3y = 13

3y = 9

y = 3

5. Probability [4]

a) Total balls:

Red + Green + Yellow = 4 + 6 + 5 = 15 balls

b) P(green ball):

P(green) = Number of green balls / Total balls

= 6/15 = 2/5

c) P(NOT red ball):

P(not red) = 1 - P(red) = 1 - 4/15 = 11/15

Or: P(not red) = (6 + 5)/15 = 11/15

d) P(red OR yellow):

P(red or yellow) = (4 + 5)/15 = 9/15 = 3/5

e) Sum of all probabilities:

P(red) + P(green) + P(yellow) = 4/15 + 6/15 + 5/15 = 15/15 = 1

(The sum of probabilities of all outcomes always equals 1)
