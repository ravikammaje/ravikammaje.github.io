1. Rational Numbers:

a) Arrange in ascending order: -2/3, 5/6, -3/4, 1/2

b) Find the value of: (-2/5) x (3/7) + (-2/5) x (4/7)

c) The product of two rational numbers is -8/9. If one of them is -4/3, find the other.

d) Find a rational number between 1/4 and 1/3. [4]

2. Percentage, Profit and Loss:

a) 35% of a number is 175. Find the number.

b) A trader buys goods for Rs 5,000 and sells them for Rs 4,500. Find the loss percentage.

c) By selling an article for Rs 720, a merchant gains 20%. Find the cost price of the article.

d) What percentage is 45 minutes of 3 hours? [4]

3. Area of Plane Figures:

5. Probability:
