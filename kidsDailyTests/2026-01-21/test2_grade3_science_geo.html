Section C:

1. Water from rivers and oceans evaporates due to heat. Water vapor rises and forms clouds. When clouds get heavy, water falls as rain.
2. Accept correctly labeled diagram with roots, stem, leaves, flower.
3. Village: fewer people, farming, less traffic. City: many people, offices/factories, more vehicles, tall buildings.
4. Birds migrate to warmer places; Bears hibernate in winter; Animals grow thicker fur in cold weather.
5. Examples: Maharashtra–Mumbai, Karnataka–Bengaluru, Tamil Nadu–Chennai, Kerala–Thiruvananthapuram, Delhi–New Delhi.
