Section C: Long Answer Questions (5 x 4 = 20 Marks)

Show all your working clearly.

1. Place Value and Number Names:

a) Write the expanded form of 6,384.

b) Which digit is in the hundreds place?

c) Write a 4-digit number where the digit 5 is in the thousands place.

d) Arrange these numbers in ascending order: 4,521, 4,152, 4,512, 4,215. [4]

2. 2D Shapes and Their Properties:

a) Draw a triangle, rectangle, and pentagon. Write the number of sides each has.

b) Which shape has no corners? Name it and draw it.

c) How many lines of symmetry does a square have? Draw and show them.

d) Name two things in your classroom that are shaped like a rectangle. [4]

3. Multiplication Word Problems:

a) There are 8 rows of chairs in a classroom. Each row has 7 chairs. How many chairs are there in total?

b) A baker makes 12 cupcakes every hour. How many cupcakes does he make in 6 hours?

c) A book has 11 pages. If a library has 9 such books, how many pages are there in total?

d) Tickets cost Rs 8 each. How much will 11 tickets cost? [4]

4. Measurement - Weight:

A shopkeeper sells fruits. He has: Apples - 2 kg 500 g, Oranges - 1 kg 750 g, Bananas - 3 kg 200 g

a) Convert all weights to grams.

b) Which fruit is the heaviest? Which is the lightest?

c) What is the total weight of all fruits in grams?

d) If a customer buys 500 g of apples, how many grams of apples are left? [4]

5. Number Patterns:

Look at this pattern: 3, 6, 9, 12, 15, ...

a) What are the next three numbers in this pattern?

b) What is the rule for this pattern?

c) Is 27 part of this pattern? Explain why or why not.

d) Create your own pattern starting with 4, where you add 5 each time. Write the first 6 numbers. [4]
