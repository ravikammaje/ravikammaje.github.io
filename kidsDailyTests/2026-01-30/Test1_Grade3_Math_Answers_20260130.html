Section A: Multiple Choice Questions (5 x 2 = 10 Marks)

1. What is the place value of 7 in 4,726?

Answer: c) 700

The 7 is in the hundreds place, so its value is 700.

2. Which shape has 4 equal sides and 4 right angles?

Answer: b) Square

A square has all sides equal and all angles 90 degrees.

3. What is 7 x 8?

Answer: b) 56

7 x 8 = 56

4. 1 kilogram = ? grams

Answer: c) 1000 grams

1 kg = 1000 g

5. What comes next: 5, 10, 15, 20, ___?

Answer: c) 25

Pattern adds 5 each time: 20 + 5 = 25

Section B: Short Answer Questions (5 x 2 = 10 Marks)

1. Write the number name for 8,459 and identify the place value of each digit. [2]

Answer:

Number name: Eight thousand four hundred fifty-nine

Place values: 8 = Thousands (8000), 4 = Hundreds (400), 5 = Tens (50), 9 = Ones (9)

2. Draw a rectangle and mark its length and breadth. How many corners does it have? [2]

Answer:

A rectangle has 4 corners (vertices).

The longer side is called length, the shorter side is called breadth.

3. Find the product: 9 x 12 = ? [2]

Answer:

9 x 12 = 9 x (10 + 2) = 90 + 18 = 108

Or: 9 x 12 = 108

4. A watermelon weighs 3 kg 250 g. Express this weight in grams only. [2]

Answer:

3 kg = 3 x 1000 g = 3000 g

3 kg 250 g = 3000 g + 250 g = 3250 g

5. Complete the pattern: 2, 4, 8, 16, ___, ___. What rule is being followed? [2]

Answer:

Next numbers: 32, 64

Rule: Each number is doubled (multiplied by 2)

2 x 2 = 4, 4 x 2 = 8, 8 x 2 = 16, 16 x 2 = 32, 32 x 2 = 64

2. 2D Shapes and Their Properties: [4]

Answers:

a) Triangle = 3 sides, Rectangle = 4 sides, Pentagon = 5 sides

b) A circle has no corners.

c) A square has 4 lines of symmetry (2 diagonal and 2 through the middle of opposite sides).

d) Examples: blackboard, door, notebook, window, desk top (any two)

4. Measurement - Weight: [4]

Answers:

a) Conversions to grams:

Apples: 2 kg 500 g = 2000 + 500 = 2500 g

Oranges: 1 kg 750 g = 1000 + 750 = 1750 g

Bananas: 3 kg 200 g = 3000 + 200 = 3200 g

b) Heaviest: Bananas (3200 g), Lightest: Oranges (1750 g)

c) Total weight = 2500 + 1750 + 3200 = 7450 g

d) Apples left = 2500 - 500 = 2000 g
