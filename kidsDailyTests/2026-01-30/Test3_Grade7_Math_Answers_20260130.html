Section B: Short Answer Questions (5 x 2 = 10 Marks)

1. Simplify: 34 × 32 ÷ 33 [2]

Answer:

34 × 32 ÷ 33

= 3(4+2) ÷ 33

= 36 ÷ 33

= 3(6-3)

= 33 = 27

2. Find the simple interest and amount on Rs 8000 at 6% per annum for 3 years. [2]

Answer:

SI = (P × R × T)/100 = (8000 × 6 × 3)/100 = Rs 1440

Amount = P + SI = 8000 + 1440 = Rs 9440

3. Two complementary angles are in the ratio 2:3. Find the angles. [2]

Answer:

Complementary angles sum to 90°

Let angles be 2x and 3x

2x + 3x = 90°

5x = 90°

x = 18°

Angles are: 2 × 18° = 36° and 3 × 18° = 54°

4. Divide Rs 350 between A and B in the ratio 3:4. [2]

Answer:

Total parts = 3 + 4 = 7

A's share = (3/7) × 350 = Rs 150

B's share = (4/7) × 350 = Rs 200

5. Find the perimeter of a rectangle with length 12 cm and breadth 8 cm. [2]

Answer:

Perimeter = 2(length + breadth)

= 2(12 + 8)

= 2 × 20

= 40 cm

2. Simple Interest: [4]

Answers:

a) P = Rs 12,000, R = 9%, T = 4 years

SI = (12000 × 9 × 4)/100 = Rs 4320

Amount = 12000 + 4320 = Rs 16,320

b) P = Rs 5000, A = Rs 6500, T = 3 years

SI = 6500 - 5000 = Rs 1500

R = (SI × 100)/(P × T) = (1500 × 100)/(5000 × 3) = 10%

c) For money to double: A = 2P, so SI = P

P = (P × 10 × T)/100

100 = 10T

T = 10 years

d) SI = Rs 1200, T = 3 years, R = 8%

P = (SI × 100)/(R × T) = (1200 × 100)/(8 × 3) = Rs 5000

3. Angles and Parallel Lines: [4]

Answers:

a) Complementary angles: Two angles that add up to 90°. Example: 30° and 60°

Supplementary angles: Two angles that add up to 180°. Example: 110° and 70°

b) Complement of 37° = 90° - 37° = 53°

Supplement of 118° = 180° - 118° = 62°

c) Let the angles be x and 3x

x + 3x = 180° (supplementary)

4x = 180°

x = 45°

The angles are 45° and 135°

d) When parallel lines are cut by a transversal with one angle = 70°:

Corresponding angles: 70°

Alternate interior angles: 70°

Co-interior angles: 180° - 70° = 110°

Vertically opposite to 70°: 70°

The 8 angles are: 70°, 110°, 70°, 110° (at one point) and 70°, 110°, 70°, 110° (at the other point)

4. Ratio, Proportion and Unitary Method: [4]

Answers:

a) In 4 hours: 240 km

In 1 hour: 240/4 = 60 km

In 7 hours: 60 × 7 = 420 km

b) 8 workers → 15 days

1 worker → 15 × 8 = 120 days (inverse proportion)

12 workers → 120/12 = 10 days

c) Total parts = 2 + 3 + 4 = 9

P's share = (2/9) × 1800 = Rs 400

Q's share = (3/9) × 1800 = Rs 600

R's share = (4/9) × 1800 = Rs 800

d) Cost of 12 pens = Rs 180

Cost of 1 pen = 180/12 = Rs 15

Cost of 20 pens = 15 × 20 = Rs 300

5. Perimeter and Area: [4]

Answers:

a) Perimeter of garden = 2(length + breadth)

= 2(50 + 30) = 2 × 80 = 160 m

b) Area of garden = length × breadth

= 50 × 30 = 1500 m²

c) Inner dimensions (after 2m path on each side):

Inner length = 50 - 2 - 2 = 46 m

Inner breadth = 30 - 2 - 2 = 26 m

Inner area = 46 × 26 = 1196 m²

Area of path = 1500 - 1196 = 304 m²

d) Cost of fencing = Perimeter × Rate per meter

= 160 × 25 = Rs 4000
