Section C: Long Answer Questions (5 x 4 = 20 Marks)

Answer the following questions in detail.

1. Reflection of Light: Physics [4]

a) Define the following terms: incident ray, reflected ray, normal, angle of incidence.

b) Draw a neat diagram showing the reflection of light from a plane mirror. Label all the parts.

c) State the two laws of reflection.

d) Why can we see a clear image in a plane mirror but not on a rough surface like a wall?

2. Separation of Mixtures: Chemistry [4]

a) What is a mixture? How is it different from a compound?

b) Describe how you would separate a mixture of sand and salt. Write all the steps.

c) What is the principle behind the separation technique of distillation?

d) Name the technique used to separate: (i) cream from milk (ii) different colored dyes from ink

3. Human Nervous System: Biology [4]

a) What is the nervous system? Name its two main divisions.

b) Draw a simple diagram of the human brain and label: cerebrum, cerebellum, and medulla oblongata.

c) What are the functions of: (i) cerebrum (ii) cerebellum (iii) medulla oblongata?

d) What is a reflex action? Give one example.

4. Metals and Non-Metals: Chemistry [4]

a) List four physical properties of metals.

b) List four physical properties of non-metals.

c) What is corrosion? How does rusting of iron occur?

d) Write two methods to prevent rusting of iron.

5. Excretory System: Biology [4]

a) What is excretion? Why is it important for the body?

b) Name the main organs of the human excretory system.

c) Draw a labeled diagram of a kidney (external view) and label: cortex, medulla, renal artery, renal vein, ureter.
