11. Answer: The three consecutive integers are 23, 24, and 25
Working:
Let the first number be x
Then the three consecutive numbers are: x, x+1, x+2
Sum = x + (x+1) + (x+2) = 72
3x + 3 = 72
3x = 72 - 3
3x = 69
x = 23
Therefore, the three numbers are: 23, 24, 25
12.
a) Total cost price = ₹3,000
b) Total selling price = ₹3,840
c) Total profit = ₹840
d) Profit percentage = 28%

Working:
a) Cost price of 200 pens = 200 × ₹15 = ₹3,000

b) Selling price of 120 pens = 120 × ₹20 = ₹2,400
Remaining pens = 200 - 120 = 80 pens
Selling price of 80 pens = 80 × ₹18 = ₹1,440
Total selling price = ₹2,400 + ₹1,440 = ₹3,840

c) Profit = Selling price - Cost price
Profit = ₹3,840 - ₹3,000 = ₹840

d) Profit percentage = (Profit ÷ Cost price) × 100
Profit % = (840 ÷ 3,000) × 100 = 28%

15.
a) Simple interest for 3 years = ₹2,880
b) Total amount after 3 years = ₹10,880
c) Time = 4 years

Working:
Principal (P) = ₹8,000
Rate (R) = 12% per annum

Formula: Simple Interest (SI) = (P × R × T) ÷ 100

a) For Time (T) = 3 years
SI = (8,000 × 12 × 3) ÷ 100
SI = 288,000 ÷ 100 = ₹2,880

b) Amount = Principal + Simple Interest
Amount = ₹8,000 + ₹2,880 = ₹10,880

c) Given SI = ₹3,840, find T
SI = (P × R × T) ÷ 100
3,840 = (8,000 × 12 × T) ÷ 100
3,840 = (96,000 × T) ÷ 100
3,840 = 960 × T
T = 3,840 ÷ 960
T = 4 years
