Section A: Multiple Choice Questions (5 × 2 = 10 Marks)

1. Simplify: 23 × 24
Answer: a) 27
Law: am × an = am+n
23+4 = 27
2. If a : b = 3 : 4 and b : c = 2 : 5, find a : c
Answer: b) 3 : 10
a/b × b/c = a/c
3/4 × 2/5 = 6/20 = 3/10
3. Two angles are supplementary. If one angle is 65°...
Answer: c) 115°
Supplementary = 180°
180° - 65° = 115°
4. A train travels 240 km in 3 hours...
Answer: c) 80 km/h
Speed = Distance/Time = 240/3 = 80 km/h
5. Circumference of circle with r = 7 cm
Answer: b) 44 cm

Section B: Short Answer Questions (5 × 2 = 10 Marks)

6. Simplify: (52)3 ÷ 54
Answer:
Using law (am)n = amn:
(52)3 = 52×3 = 56

Using law am ÷ an = am-n:
56 ÷ 54 = 56-4 = 52 = 25
7. If 12 pens cost ₹180, find the cost of 20 pens.
Answer:
Cost of 12 pens = ₹180
Cost of 1 pen = ₹180 ÷ 12 = ₹15
Cost of 20 pens = ₹15 × 20 = ₹300
8. Find the mean: 15, 20, 18, 22, 25
Answer:
Sum = 15 + 20 + 18 + 22 + 25 = 100
Number of values = 5
Mean = Sum ÷ Number of values = 100 ÷ 5 = 20
9. In a parallelogram, one angle is 70°. Find the other three angles.
Answer:
In a parallelogram:
- Opposite angles are equal
- Adjacent angles are supplementary (sum = 180°)

Given angle = 70°
Opposite angle = 70°
Adjacent angles = 180° - 70° = 110° each

The four angles are: 70°, 110°, 70°, 110°
10. A car travels at 60 km/h. Time to cover 210 km?
Answer:
Speed = 60 km/h
Distance = 210 km
Time = Distance ÷ Speed = 210 ÷ 60 = 3.5 hours = 3 hours 30 minutes

Section C: Long Answer Questions (5 × 4 = 20 Marks)

11. Simple Interest problem
Answer:
Principal (P) = ₹15,000
Rate (R) = 8% per annum
Time (T) = 3 years

a) Simple Interest = (P × R × T) / 100
SI = (15000 × 8 × 3) / 100 = 360000 / 100 = ₹3,600

b) Amount = Principal + Simple Interest
A = 15000 + 3600 = ₹18,600

c) To earn ₹6,000 interest:
6000 = (15000 × 8 × T) / 100
6000 = 1200 × T
T = 6000 / 1200 = 5 years
12. Parallel lines cut by transversal (∠1 = 55°)
Answer:
Given: l ∥ m, transversal t, ∠1 = 55°

a) Corresponding angle:
Corresponding angles are equal when lines are parallel.
Corresponding angle = 55°

b) Alternate interior angle:
Alternate interior angles are equal when lines are parallel.
Alternate interior angle = 55°

c) Co-interior angle (same-side interior):
Co-interior angles are supplementary (sum = 180°).
Co-interior angle = 180° - 55° = 125°

d) Vertically opposite angle:
Vertically opposite angles are equal.
Vertically opposite angle = 55°
13. Ratio problem (boys : girls = 5 : 4)
Answer:
Ratio of boys to girls = 5 : 4
Total students = 450
Sum of ratio parts = 5 + 4 = 9

a) Number of boys:
Boys = (5/9) × 450 = 5 × 50 = 250 boys

b) Number of girls:
Girls = (4/9) × 450 = 4 × 50 = 200 girls

c) New ratio after 50 more girls join:
New number of girls = 200 + 50 = 250
Boys : Girls = 250 : 250 = 1 : 1
14. Area of parallelogram and circular garden
Answer:
Parallelogram:
Base = 12 cm, Height = 8 cm
Area = Base × Height = 12 × 8 = 96 cm²

Circular garden (r = 14 m, π = 22/7):

a) Circumference:
C = 2πr = 2 × (22/7) × 14 = 2 × 22 × 2 = 88 m

b) Area:
A = πr² = (22/7) × 14 × 14 = 22 × 2 × 14 = 616 m²

c) Cost of fencing:
Cost = Circumference × Rate per meter
Cost = 88 × ₹25 = ₹2,200
15. Mean, Median, Mode
Answer:
Data: 45, 52, 60, 48, 52, 55, 52, 65, 48, 73

a) Mean:
Sum = 45 + 52 + 60 + 48 + 52 + 55 + 52 + 65 + 48 + 73 = 550
Number of values = 10
Mean = 550 ÷ 10 = 55

b) Median:
Arrange in order: 45, 48, 48, 52, 52, 52, 55, 60, 65, 73
Number of values = 10 (even)
Median = Average of 5th and 6th values
Median = (52 + 52) / 2 = 104 / 2 = 52

c) Mode:
Mode = most frequently occurring value
52 appears 3 times (most frequent)
Mode = 52
