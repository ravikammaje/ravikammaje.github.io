Section A: Multiple Choice Questions (5 × 2 = 10 Marks)

1. What is the place value of 7 in 3,724?

Answer: c) 700

7 is in the hundreds place, so place value = 7 × 100 = 700

2. What is 8 × 7?

Answer: c) 56

8 × 7 = 56

3. Which shape has 4 equal sides and 4 right angles?

Answer: b) Square

A square has all sides equal and all angles 90°

4. Hour hand at 4, minute hand at 6?

Answer: b) 4:30

Minute hand at 6 = 30 minutes

5. 4 stars, 1 star = 5 books?

Answer: d) 20

4 × 5 = 20 books

Section C: Long Answer Questions (5 × 4 = 20 Marks)

1. Pencils problem:

a) Total pencils:

2,365 + 1,847 = 4,212 pencils

b) After selling 1,500:

4,212 - 1,500 = 2,712 pencils left

c) Packed into 5 boxes:

2,712 ÷ 5 = 542 pencils per box (with 2 remaining)

2. Rohan's school timing:

a) Wake up time:

Time needed = 45 min (getting ready) + 20 min (travel) = 65 min = 1 hour 5 min

8:30 AM - 1 hour 5 min = 7:25 AM

b) Hours at school:

8:30 AM to 2:15 PM = 5 hours 45 minutes

c) Hours after school starts for favorite class:

11:45 AM - 8:30 AM = 3 hours 15 minutes

3. Pictograph - Fruits sold:

a) Fruits sold (1 star = 10 fruits):

Apples: 6 × 10 = 60

Oranges: 4 × 10 = 40

Bananas: 8 × 10 = 80

Mangoes: 3 × 10 = 30

b) Most sold: Bananas (80)

Least sold: Mangoes (30)

c) Bananas - Oranges:

80 - 40 = 40 more bananas

4. Classroom desks:

a) Total desks:

12 × 8 = 96 desks

b) After removing 7 desks:

96 - 7 = 89 desks

c) Students that can sit (2 per desk):

89 × 2 = 178 students

5. Shapes:

a) Rectangle:

Has 2 pairs of equal sides (opposite sides are equal)

b) Triangle:

Has 3 sides and 3 angles

c) Hexagon:

A hexagon has 6 sides

Perimeter = 6 × 5 cm = 30 cm
