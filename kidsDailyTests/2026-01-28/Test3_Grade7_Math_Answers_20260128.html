Section B: Short Answer Questions (5 × 2 = 10 Marks)

1. Evaluate: (−12) × (−4) ÷ (−6)

SI = (5000 × 8 × 2)/100

SI = ₹800

5. Perimeter of rectangle (l=12 cm, b=7 cm).

= 2(12 + 7) = 2 × 19 = 38 cm

Section C: Long Answer Questions (5 × 4 = 20 Marks)

1. Integer operations:

a) (−15) + 8 − (−12) + (−5) − 7

= −15 + 8 + 12 − 5 − 7

= −15 − 5 − 7 + 8 + 12

= −27 + 20 = −7

b) Temperature problem:

Morning: −5°C

After rise: −5 + 12 = 7°C

After drop: 7 − 8 = −1°C

c) Elevator problem:

Start: 3

Up 7: 3 + 7 = 10

Down 12: 10 − 12 = −2

Up 5: −2 + 5 = Floor 3

2. Exponents and powers:

a) (32)3 × 3−4

= 36 × 3−4 = 36−4 = 32 = 9

b) 2x = 64

64 = 26

So x = 6

c) 0.00045 in standard form:

= 4.5 × 10−4

= 4.5 × 10−4

d) (53 × 52) ÷ 54

= 53+2 ÷ 54 = 55 ÷ 54 = 55−4 = 51 = 5

3. Simple Interest problems:

P = ₹12,000, R = 10%

a) SI for 3 years:

SI = (12000 × 10 × 3)/100 = ₹3,600

b) Amount after 3 years:

A = P + SI = 12000 + 3600 = ₹15,600

c) Time to earn ₹6,000:

6000 = (12000 × 10 × T)/100

6000 = 1200T

T = 6000/1200 = 5 years

d) Rate to earn ₹4,800 in 4 years:

4800 = (12000 × R × 4)/100

4800 = 480R

R = 4800/480 = 10%

4. Speed, Distance, Time:

a) Total distance by cyclist:

Distance 1 = 15 × 2 = 30 km

Distance 2 = 20 × 3 = 60 km

Total = 30 + 60 = 90 km

b) Train arrival time:

Time = 450/75 = 6 hours

Departure: 9:00 AM

Arrival: 9:00 AM + 6 hours = 3:00 PM

c) Car journey time:

Half distance = 120 km

Time 1 = 120/40 = 3 hours

Time 2 = 120/60 = 2 hours

Total time = 3 + 2 = 5 hours
