Section C: Long Answer Questions (5 × 4 = 20 Marks)

Answer the following questions in detail.

1. Living things have special characteristics that non-living things do not have. Science [4]

a) Name four characteristics of living things.

b) A car moves and uses fuel for energy. Is it a living thing? Explain why or why not.

c) Give two examples each of living things and non-living things found in a garden.

2. Our Earth has continents and oceans. Geography [4]

a) Name all seven continents.

b) Which is the largest continent? Which is the smallest?

c) Which continent do we live in?

d) Name the ocean that lies to the south of India.

3. Insects are very important creatures in nature. Science [4]

a) Draw and label the three body parts of an insect.

b) Name two useful insects and tell why they are useful.

c) Name two harmful insects and tell why they are harmful.

d) Describe the life cycle of a butterfly (4 stages).

4. India has many national symbols that represent our country. Geography [4]

a) What is the national animal of India?

b) What is the national flower of India?

c) Describe the Indian national flag (colors and what they represent).

d) What is the national emblem of India called? Where can you see it?

5. The water cycle is an important process in nature. Science [4]

a) What is evaporation? Where does the water go when it evaporates?

b) What is condensation? How do clouds form?

c) What is precipitation? Name two forms of precipitation.

d) Why is the water cycle important for life on Earth?
