Section C: Long Answer Questions (5 × 4 = 20 Marks)

Show all your working clearly.

1. A shop has 2,365 red pencils and 1,847 blue pencils.

a) How many pencils are there in total?

b) If 1,500 pencils are sold, how many are left?

c) If the remaining pencils are packed equally into 5 boxes, how many pencils will be in each box? [4]

2. Rohan's school starts at 8:30 AM. He takes 45 minutes to get ready and 20 minutes to travel to school.

a) At what time should Rohan wake up to reach school on time?

b) If school ends at 2:15 PM, how many hours and minutes is Rohan at school?

c) Rohan's favorite class is at 11:45 AM. How many hours after school starts is that? [4]

c) How many more bananas were sold than oranges? [4]

a) How many desks are there in total?

b) If 7 desks are removed for repairs, how many remain?

c) If 2 students can sit at each desk, how many students can sit in the classroom after the repairs? [4]
