ANSWER KEY - GRADE 7 MATHEMATICS

Section A: Multiple Choice Questions (10 marks)

1. b) 40
Solution: When multiplying two negative numbers, the result is positive.
(-5) × (-8) = 40

2. c) 5
Solution:
3x + 7 = 22
3x = 22 - 7
3x = 15
x = 15 ÷ 3
x = 5

3. b) 20
Solution: 25% of 80 = (25/100) × 80 = (1/4) × 80 = 20

4. b) 180°
The sum of all interior angles in any triangle is always 180°.

5. c) 29
A prime number has only two factors: 1 and itself.
29 is only divisible by 1 and 29, so it is prime.
(21 = 3×7, 27 = 3×9, 33 = 3×11 - all composite)

Section B: Short Answer Questions (10 marks)

6. 17/12 or 1 5/12
Solution:
3/4 + 2/3
LCM of 4 and 3 = 12
= (3×3)/(4×3) + (2×4)/(3×4)
= 9/12 + 8/12
= 17/12 or 1 5/12

7. 17
Solution:
2³ + 3² = (2×2×2) + (3×3)
= 8 + 9
= 17

8. 3/4
Solution:

= 3/4

9. 12 cm
Solution:
Perimeter of square = 4 × side
48 = 4 × side
side = 48 ÷ 4 = 12 cm

10. 18
Solution:
Average = Sum of all values ÷ Number of values
= (12 + 15 + 18 + 21 + 24) ÷ 5
= 90 ÷ 5
= 18

Section C: Long Answer Questions (20 marks)

11. x = 9
Solution:
5(x - 3) = 2(x + 6)
5x - 15 = 2x + 12
5x - 2x = 12 + 15
3x = 27
x = 9
Verification:
LHS: 5(9 - 3) = 5(6) = 30
RHS: 2(9 + 6) = 2(15) = 30
LHS = RHS, hence verified ✓

12. a) Profit = ₹360, b) Selling Price = ₹2,760
Solution:
Cost Price (CP) = ₹2,400
Profit% = 15%
a) Profit amount:
Profit = (15/100) × 2,400
= (15 × 2,400)/100
= ₹360
b) Selling Price:
SP = CP + Profit
= ₹2,400 + ₹360
= ₹2,760

13. a) 10 girls, b) 25 students
Solution:
Ratio of boys to girls = 3:2
Number of boys = 15
a) Number of girls:
If 3 parts = 15 boys
Then 1 part = 15 ÷ 3 = 5
Girls = 2 parts = 2 × 5 = 10 girls
b) Total students:
Total = Boys + Girls
= 15 + 10
= 25 students

14. a) Area = 216 m², b) Cost = ₹1,500
Solution:
Length = 18 m, Width = 12 m
a) Area:
Area = Length × Width
= 18 × 12
= 216 m²
b) Cost of fencing:
Perimeter = 2(Length + Width)
= 2(18 + 12)
= 2(30)
= 60 m
Cost = 60 × ₹25 = ₹1,500

15. a) 100 students, b) 45%, c) Bar graph representation
Solution:
a) Total students:
Total = 45 + 30 + 15 + 10 = 100 students
b) Percentage preferring Cricket:
Percentage = (45/100) × 100 = 45%
c) Bar graph: Students should draw a bar graph with:
- X-axis: Sports (Cricket, Football, Tennis, Others)
- Y-axis: Number of students (scale: 0-50)
- Bars of heights: Cricket-45, Football-30, Tennis-15, Others-10
(Award marks for correct total, percentage calculation, and reasonable representation)
