Section A: Multiple Choice Questions (10 marks)

Section C: Long Answer Questions (20 marks)

11. 8 marbles each
Solution: 48 ÷ 6 = 8
Each friend will get 8 marbles.

12. 405 apples
Solution:
Monday: 135 apples
Tuesday: 142 apples
Wednesday: 128 apples
Total = 135 + 142 + 128 = 405 apples

13. 5:45
Solution: Starting time is 3:15
Add 2 hours: 3:15 + 2:00 = 5:15
Add 30 minutes: 5:15 + 0:30 = 5:45

14. Total eggs = 63, Eggs left = 48
Solution:
Total eggs = 7 × 9 = 63 eggs
After selling 15 eggs: 63 - 15 = 48 eggs left

15. Yes, you can buy both items. Money left = ₹27
Solution:
Total cost = ₹125 + ₹48 = ₹173
Money available = ₹200
Since ₹200 > ₹173, yes you can buy both.
Money left = ₹200 - ₹173 = ₹27
