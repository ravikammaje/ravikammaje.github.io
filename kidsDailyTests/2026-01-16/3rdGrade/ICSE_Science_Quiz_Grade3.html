Section B: Short Answer Guidelines
1. Water: Essential for survival. Plants use it for photosynthesis and nutrient transport. Animals need it for digestion, temperature regulation, and waste removal.
2. Germination: Seeds need water, warmth, and air. The seed coat softens, a root grows down, and a shoot grows up toward light.
3. Fish vs Bird: Fish: water, gills, scales/fins, cold-blooded. Birds: land/air, lungs, feathers/wings, warm-blooded.
4. Water Cycle: Sun heats water ‚Üí evaporates ‚Üí rises as vapor ‚Üí cools into clouds ‚Üí falls as rain/snow ‚Üí cycle repeats.
5. Nutrition: Vitamin A (carrots): eye health. Vitamin C (oranges): healing/immunity. Vitamin K (spinach): blood clotting.
